Game Introduction

In " Beijing Life ", you play a young man who comes to Beijing from other places to make a living. At the beginning, you only have 2,000 yuan, and you still owe the village chief (a gangster leader) 5,500 yuan. The interest on these debts is as high as 10% per day. If you don't pay it off in time, the village chief will ask his fellow villagers in Beijing to beat you up, and you may die on the streets of Beijing. You decide to make a fortune by reselling various items in the black markets in the Beijing subway . Not only do you pay off your debts, but you also want to be on the list of Beijing's richest people. You can only stay in Beijing for 40 days, and each visit to a black market counts as one day. At the beginning, the house you rent can only hold 100 things. You will encounter various events in Beijing , which will make you feel the hardship of life in Beijing, the fickleness of the world, and the strong sense of absurdity of our time.

Features of "Beijing Floating Life"

  • 1. Highly playable and interesting, with a strong Beijing local background and spicy comedy style.
  • 2. Play for a shorter period of time to adapt to the fast-paced era.
  • 3. The interface is specially designed, and it doesn't look like a game at first glance. It also has a special "Boss is coming" protection. Therefore, it is difficult for your boss to find out if you are playing during work hours.
  • 4. The events and item prices in the game are random, giving you a fair, just and passionate space for overnight explosion.

Therefore, "Beijing Fu Sheng Ji" is an ideal game for you to have fun after work or study. Office workers can also use it to change their minds while working. All you need is strategy , courage and luck!

Gameplay

The gameplay of "Beijing Fu Sheng Ji" is very simple and easy to get started. The basic operation process is as follows:

  • 1. Buy appropriate items (it can be one or more) from the black market in a certain place (such as Xizhimen ).
  • 2. Go to another place (such as Fuxingmen ). You only need to click the place name on the interface to go there.
  • 3. Observe the black market in the new location. If the price of an item is higher than your purchase price, you can sell the item from your rental house, so you make money. Then buy other suitable items from the black market in the new location.
  • 4. Repeat the above steps 1, 2, and 3.

    • Finally, the game counts your total assets (cash + deposits - debts) and calculates your ranking in the Beijing Rich List. Your goal is to make more money and strive to enter the top ten.

It should be noted that one day of running from one place to another is counted in the game, and you can only stay in Beijing for 40 days in total. Therefore, you must seize every day, master the buying and selling strategies, and earn the maximum profit.

As the saying goes, "It's easy to get started, but hard to improve." The basic gameplay is simple, but it's not easy and challenging to make a lot of money (for example, break through 30 million) in 40 days. You need:

  • 1. Have a basic understanding of the prices of various items , so that you can "buy low and sell high". For example, if the price of imported toys is below 350 yuan, you can make money by buying them. How to grasp the basic price of items? This requires you to study diligently, play more times, and be a caring person.
  • Second, fully grasp the business opportunities brought by various random events in the game . Many random events in the game will affect the price of items. For example, the event "The market is full of smuggled cigarettes from Fujian!" will make the price of cigarettes depressed. At this time, you can buy imported cigarettes in large quantities, and you may make a lot of money the next day. For another example, when the event "Rich people in Beijing are crazy about buying smuggled cars! Prices are soaring!" occurs, you should sell the smuggled cars immediately, and you will definitely make a lot of money.
  • 3. Timely expansion of business scale . This has two meanings: First, after you have accumulated a certain amount of funds by reselling cheap items (such as pirated VCDs), you should turn to reselling expensive items, such as "Shanghai Baby" and smuggled cars, etc., whose prices have a large room for change, giving you the opportunity to make a lot of money; second, when you have enough money, you should rent a bigger house from a rental agency, because at the beginning your house can only hold 100 items. In order to expand your business, you should rent a house that can hold more items.
  • 4. Use other strategies . Please refer to the strategy section for details .

In-game events

Random events make "Beijing Floating Life" full of randomness, challenges and fun. Every time you play, the situation is completely different. There are three types of random events in the game:

  • 1. Business events , which affect the price of items and the quantity of items you bring. For example, the event "College students in Beijing are starting to look for jobs, and parallel-imported mobile phones are very popular!" can cause the price of mobile phones to soar by 7 times.
  • Second, health events affect your physical health. For example, a sandstorm in Beijing will reduce your health.
  • 3. Money-losing events can reduce your hard-earned money. For example, an old lady wearing a red armband in Beijing will fine you because you are from another place, and a person pretending to be a beggar at the subway entrance will make you donate a large sum of money.

In this case, how to make money by using business events and avoid losing money becomes a very important strategy in the game. All the events in the game are analyzed in detail later .

    • Strategy Guide

    In "Beijing Floating Life", luck is important, but the right strategy is the decisive factor. The general strategy is as follows, but more tricks are waiting for you to discover in practice.

  • 1. Rely on banks and save money in time

    • The streets of Beijing in the game are seriously polluted, and the social security situation is deteriorating, which is very dangerous. There are murderers lurking in the underground passages, migrant workers fighting collectively at construction sites, thieves and robbers wandering around, Aunt Wang who discriminates against immigrants, and fake beggars who cheat you of your money. How to protect the safety of your property? Banks are an ideal place. The interest rate of banks is very low, only 1%, but keeping money in banks can avoid the impact of most money-losing events on your cash and give you a sense of security.
  • 2. Take things step by step and take risks.

    • At the beginning, you can resell cheap things (such as VCD) for primitive accumulation. After you have a certain economic foundation, you can resell expensive things (such as smuggled cars). Although there are greater risks, the profit margins will also increase accordingly.
  • 3. Diversify operations to reduce risks.

    • Risks always exist. When economic conditions permit, you can import multiple goods at the same time to transform the risks.
  • 4. Don’t be greedy or stubborn, a brave man cuts off his arm.

    • Greed and obsession are taboos for making money. Therefore, when you find valuable low-priced goods (such as a car for only 15,000 yuan), and your rental house is full of unprofitable items, you would rather lose money and sell the items at a low price, and buy valuable low-priced goods in time.
  • 5. Take care of yourself and your health.

    • Your health is important. Don't sacrifice it on the streets of Beijing. When your health declines, seek timely treatment.
  • 6. Pay attention to reputation and gain both fame and fortune.

    • Reselling fake liquor and the banned book "Shanghai Baby" can make you money quickly, but it will have a big impact on your reputation. No matter how much money you have, you will not be a successful businessman if you have a bad reputation, so try not to buy and sell fake liquor and other products.
  • 7. Expand your business

    • Specifically, if your financial conditions allow, you can rent a larger house from a rental agency and resell things. However, the agency will often cheat you out of some money.
  • 8. Don’t be afraid of difficulties and study diligently.

    • The characteristic of this game is "easy to get started, difficult to improve." It is easy to earn 100,000 yuan, but difficult to earn 30 million yuan. Beginners often find it difficult to grasp the money-making strategy and earn very little money, but don't give up. Play it again and you may make a profit. Play more and think more, you will definitely have more experience, and you will definitely become a master who earns more than 20 million yuan. At present, there are masters who have earned 180 million yuan.
